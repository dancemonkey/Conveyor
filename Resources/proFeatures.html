Why Go Pro?

Besides supporting the continued development of Conveyor and enabling the team to continue to add new features to the app, you get a number of new features only available to Pro users.

  • Repeating tasks: Include the tag "@rpt" in your task text to mark it as repeating. Repeating tasks have a small indicator icon to show that they repeat. Now whenever you complete the task it will re-schedule itself for tomorrow.
  • Prioritize tasks: Include the tag "@!" to mark a task as high-priority. High-priority tasks have a small indicator icon to show their high priority status, and also float to the top of the list.
  • More! We will continue to develop and add new features for both free and pro users.
